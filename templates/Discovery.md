## Review Checklist

### Discovery Review Criteria

- [ ] **Clarity**: Problem statement is clear and unambiguous
- [ ] **Alignment**: Business objectives align with organizational strategy
- [ ] **Stakeholders**: All key stakeholders identified and engaged
- [ ] **Feasibility**: Initial feasibility assessment completed
- [ ] **Scope**: High-level scope is defined and boundaries are clear
- [ ] **Metrics**: Success criteria are measurable and achievable
- [ ] **Risks**: Major risks identified with mitigation strategies
- [ ] **Approval**: Business sponsor has approved proceeding to Design
